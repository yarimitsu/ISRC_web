Conduct Research on the Island

Scott and our team of researchers have decades of experience working on Middleton Island. We welcome collaborations with scientists studying seabird ecology, marine ecosystems, climate change impacts, and related fields.

The station offers unique opportunities for:

  • Long-term population monitoring studies
  • Experimental manipulations in controlled environments
  • Behavioral and physiological research
  • Student training and career development
  • Cross-disciplinary ecosystem research

Location

Find Middleton Island

Middleton Island is located approximately 60 miles offshore from the south-central Alaskan coast in the Gulf of Alaska. The island is accessible only by aircraft via the former Air Force airstrip.
